About eGift cards

A Moon's Coffee eGift card (also known as an "eGift") is a gift card that is purchased and sent digitally.

For Senders of an eGift, go to eGift History when signed in to view, send, or resend eGifts you've purchased — or, to directly contact eGift Support for help with your order.

About Moon's Coffee Gift Cards in general

Moon's Coffee Gift Cards can be used to pay in a participating Moon's Coffee store or can be used to join the Moon's Coffee Rewards loyalty program.

Register Gift Cards and eGifts to a Moon's Coffee Rewards account and Rewards members earn Stars on qualifying purchases when you pay with that card.
