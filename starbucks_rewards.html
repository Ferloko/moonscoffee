Terms of use

For full program details visit starbucks.com/rewards/terms.

Starbucks® Rewards benefits are available at participating Moon's Coffee stores. Not all stores have the ability to honor Rewards at this time. Visit the Starbucks Store Locator and search for locations honoring "Redeem Rewards".

Earn rate

  • Green members earn 1 Star per $1 spent at participating stores.
  • Gold members earn 1.2 Stars per $1 spent at participating stores.
  • Reserve members earn 1.7 Stars per $1 spent at participating stores.

Birthday Reward

  • Green members receive a Birthday Reward valid for 1 day.
  • Gold members receive a Birthday Reward valid for 7 days.
  • Reserve members receive a Birthday Reward valid for 30 days.

Redeeming Rewards

Food and beverage Rewards cannot be combined with other offers or discounts. Some restrictions apply.

Mod Monday

One free drink customization per month for eligible members at participating stores.

Star expiration: Green members' Stars expire 6 months after the calendar month in which they were earned. Gold and Reserve members' Stars do not expire as long as the member remains in that status level.

Partnerships: See Delta Terms and Conditions and Marriott Bonvoy Terms and Conditions.
